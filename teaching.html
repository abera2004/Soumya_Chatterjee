Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach emphasizes a strong foundation of electrical engineering principles with real-world applications. I integrate problem-based learning, simulation tools, and research insights to encourage critical thinking and innovation. The goal is to empower students to apply theory into practice across power systems, high-voltage equipment, and advanced signal processing domains.
